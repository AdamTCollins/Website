Goals

  • Rid the oceans of plastic
  • Spread awareness of pollution within the oceans
  • Stop overfishing









Our oceans and seas are constantly being littered with plastic and
polluted. If you look at the image above, you can see the build-up
of plastic material in the middle of the Pacific ocean.





We have to act now to reduce plastic pollution, preserving wildlife
and stopping the destruction of our oceans. Our oceans are being
exploited and destroyed at an alarming rate






Overfishing is major factor in the deterioration of reefs and culling
of large shoals of fish, including sharks and whales
Overfishing is possible due to super trawlers that can stay at sea for
months, large nets and sonar
